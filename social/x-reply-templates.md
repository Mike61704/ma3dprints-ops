# X – Reply Templates for MA 3D Prints

Short, reusable replies you can drop into relevant conversations.

## When someone mentions a broken plastic part

> This is exactly what I help with locally in New Braunfels.
> I run MA 3D Prints – I can usually recreate small plastic parts and print replacements.
> If you’ve got a photo + rough measurements, there’s a good chance it can be fixed instead of tossed.

Shorter version:

> I run a small 3D printing shop in New Braunfels (MA 3D Prints).
> I can often recreate and reprint small broken plastic parts locally.

## When someone is asking about 3D printing services

> If you’re near New Braunfels, I run MA 3D Prints – a local 3D printing service.
> I handle functional parts, prototypes, and toys with quick turnaround.
> Happy to take a look if you want to DM details or visit ma3dprints.com.

## When someone posts a cool 3D print / maker project

> Very cool build.
> I run a small 3D printing shop in New Braunfels and love seeing projects like this.
> Inspiring to see what people are making.

Or lighter:

> Love this.
> I run MA 3D Prints in New Braunfels and it’s awesome seeing what people make with 3D printing.

## When engaging local community / small biz posts

> Love seeing local businesses doing cool things in New Braunfels.
> I run a small 3D printing shop here (MA 3D Prints) and it’s great being part of the community.

Shorter:

> Love this – greetings from a small 3D printing shop in New Braunfels (MA 3D Prints).

---

**Usage:**
- Don’t spam these everywhere. Use them when a post is genuinely relevant.
- Feel free to tweak wording to sound more like you.
- Jason can draft situation-specific replies based on the exact tweet; these are just starting points.
